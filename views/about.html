Palace formed as a 4 piece band on 15 September 2012 and comprised Leo Wyndham, Rupert Turner, Will Dorey, and Matt Hodges. They released their debut EP titled Lost in the Night. In 2015, Palace released their second EP titled Chase The Light.[4] In 2016, Palace released their first full-length album titled So Long Forever.Will Dorey left the band in 2017, now cultivating his new musical project called Skinshape. In 2019, the now newly 3 piece band released their second full-length album titled Life After. On 21 January 2022, Palace released their third full-length album titled Shoals.
